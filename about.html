About Us

Finpur.com is focused on Islamic business, economics and finance topics to empower individuals, businesses, investors and researchers by providing insights and tools for navigating the world of finance and ethical practices and businesses and finance.

Its mission is to create awareness regarding shariah compliant business practices. Finpur is built on the foundation of ethical business principles which emphasize fairness in business dealings, prohibition of Riba (usury) and sharing of risks and rewards focusing on community well-being and economic justice. Finpur translates these ideals into actionable insights for modern businesses, entrepreneurs and scholars.

Finpur is for entrepreneurs looking for ethical business ideas, for businesses looking for ethical business practices and shariah compliant finance opportunities, for investors willing to support shariah complaints businesses, for students and researchers exploring Islamic business, economics and finance and trends of them.

Finpur envisions a world where financial systems contribute positively to society, promoting wealth distribution and social equity.
